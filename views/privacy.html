Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 25, 2026

Overview

Expense Lite AI collects and stores the information you provide to run your account, manage your financial records, and deliver features like expenses, loans, planner data, reports, and AI lookup.

Information We Collect

  • Account details such as name, username, email address, phone number, and profile picture.
  • Financial records you add, including expenses, EMI schedules, credit card details, bank balances, planner entries, friend loans, and recurring items.
  • Technical information needed for security, login sessions, and service operation.

How We Use Information

  • To authenticate your account and protect access to your data.
  • To provide app features, reports, exports, reminders, and planning views.
  • To support AI lookup requests when you choose to use that feature.
  • To improve reliability, troubleshoot issues, and prevent abuse.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data when required to operate the service, comply with law, or fulfill a feature you explicitly use, such as AI lookup or public sharing links created by you.

Data Storage and Security

Your information is stored on the application server and protected using access controls, password hashing, and session security. No system can guarantee perfect security, but reasonable safeguards are used to protect your data.

Your Choices

You can update your profile details, change your password, and manage most of your financial records from inside the app. If you want account deletion or a privacy request, contact us using the details on the Contact page.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact expenselyt@gmail.com.
